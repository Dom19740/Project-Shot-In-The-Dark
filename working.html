SHOT IN THE DARK

One simple game, hundreds of eccentric questions...

The ultimate quiz leveller, nobody knows the answers...

Those who can't make an educated guess...

can take a shot in the dark.

Age: 12+
Players: 2+

Before starting:

  • Choose a question master to read questions and answers (or switch after each question)
  • Decide whether to play as individuals or in teams
  • Decide the score limit (or leave it blank for no limit)

To play:

  • The question master reads a question. Every player or team guesses a different answer in turn (as you play, rotate the order, the first or last guesser sometimes has an advantage)
  • The question master reads the answer. The correct answer earns a point. If there are no correct answers, the nearest guess wins a point
  • If there is no obvious nearest guess (e.g. the answer is Denmark and everybody picked countries in Africa) then nobody gets a point
  • You can skip any question
  • If there is any doubt, the question master's decision is final
  • Continue until a player or team reaches the score limit
